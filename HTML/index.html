Active Cincinnati

Cincinnati Census Tract Growth 2010-2020

and Greater Cincinnati Area Athletic clubs

by Charles Altendorf

As with many cities in America's "rust-belt", population in the city limits of Cincinnati delcined from 1950 to 2010. The trend surprisingly reversed with the publication of the 2020 Census showing a 4.2% increase.

While one can find many explanations for this reversal, an interesting one appears to be the proximity of growing census tracts (green) to private athletic facilites (blue). According to the Countywide Parks and Greenspaces layer obtained from the Cincinnati Area GIS website, these included country clubs, tennis clubs, swim clubs, and even a "sports mall"
